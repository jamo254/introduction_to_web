About us

We are a plastic collection start-up that helps individuals and businesses to properly dispose of their plastic waste in an environmentally friendly way. Our goal is to reduce plastic pollution and make the world a cleaner place for future generations.

Our Services

  • Plastic Collection

    We offer convenient and affordable plastic collection services for households and businesses. Our team picks up the plastic waste on a regular basis and ensures it is properly disposed of in a sustainable way.

  • Plastic Recycling

    We also offer plastic recycling services to ensure that the collected plastic waste is repurposed and turned into new products, reducing the amount of plastic in landfills and the environment.

  • Education and Awareness

    We believe in educating individuals and businesses on the impact of plastic pollution and how they can make a difference. We offer workshops, webinars, and other educational materials to help spread awareness and encourage action.
